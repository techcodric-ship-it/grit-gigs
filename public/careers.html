Why Grit&Gigs?

We're a small, passionate team based in Chennai with a big mission. We believe in flat hierarchies, radical transparency, and giving every team member ownership over their work. If you want to make a real impact on millions of lives, you'll fit right in.

  • Growth — We invest in your learning and career development.
  • Flexibility — Remote-friendly with a Chennai HQ. Work how you work best.
  • Impact — Your work will directly help thousands of Indians build wealth.

About us

We're building India's first platform where skills are currency and everyone can build wealth.

What is Grit&Gigs?

Grit&Gigs is India's all-in-one skill marketplace where freelancers can sell gigs, barter skills without cash, and find project partners — with escrow-protected payments, UPI/wallet support, and zero commission on barter trades. It combines freelancing, skill exchange, and project collaboration into one platform built specifically for the Indian market.

Our Mission

Grit&Gigs was founded with a simple belief: every Indian with a skill should be able to earn, grow, and invest — without barriers. We combine skill barter, freelancing, and business investment into one seamless platform so you can trade what you know for what you need.

What We Do

We offer three core pillars under one roof:

  • Skill Exchange (Barter) — Trade services directly without spending money. Your graphic design for their legal consultation. Zero cash needed.
  • Freelance Marketplace — Find paying gigs and build a client base. Post services, get hired, earn real income.
  • MicroEquity Investing — Invest small amounts in local businesses and earn returns. Coming soon.

Our Story

Founded in Chennai, India, Grit&Gigs emerged from a simple observation: talented people everywhere lack access to opportunities. Traditional platforms charge high commissions, limit who can participate, and make it hard to build real wealth. We set out to change that by building a platform where skills — not money — are the primary currency. Whether you're a student, a freelancer, a small business owner, or someone looking for a side hustle, Grit&Gigs gives you the tools to succeed on your own terms.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity — Everyone has a skill worth trading. We make sure everyone can participate, regardless of background.
  • Transparency — No hidden fees, no surprise charges. What you see is what you get.
  • Empowerment — We give you the tools to build wealth. Your success is our success.
  • Community First — We're building a community, not just a marketplace. Every transaction builds relationships.

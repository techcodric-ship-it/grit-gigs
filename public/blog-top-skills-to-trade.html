5 Skills You Can Trade Right Now on Grit&Gigs

November 22, 2025 · 3 min read

One of the most common questions we hear is: "What kind of skills actually get traded on Grit&Gigs?" The answer: almost anything. If you can teach it, design it, write it, build it, or advise on it — someone needs it. Here are the five most in-demand skill categories on the platform right now.

1. Graphic Design & Visual Branding

Logo design, social media graphics, presentation decks, brochures, and brand identity packages. Every business needs visual content, but not every business can afford a design agency. If you know Canva, Figma, Illustrator, or Photoshop, you'll never run out of barter or freelance opportunities.

How to offer it: Create a portfolio with 3–5 sample designs (even if they're mock projects). List your specific tools and styles on your Grit&Gigs profile. Specify what you want in return — web development, content writing, legal advice, etc.

2. Web Development & Technical Services

From basic HTML/CSS websites to full-stack applications, Shopify stores, WordPress setup, and landing page design. Small business owners and startup founders are constantly looking for developers — and many are willing to trade their own expertise (marketing, accounting, design) in exchange.

How to offer it: List the platforms you work with (React, Node.js, Shopify, WordPress, etc.). Include links to sites you've built. Specify whether you're open to barter, paid projects, or both.

3. Content Writing & Copywriting

Blog posts, website copy, product descriptions, email newsletters, social media captions, and SEO articles. Every online business needs words. Writers are some of the most active traders on Grit&Gigs because their skill is universally needed and easy to deliver remotely.

How to offer it: Share 2–3 writing samples. Mention your niches (tech, finance, lifestyle, B2B, etc.). If you're bilingual, highlight that — English + Hindi or English + Tamil content is in high demand.

4. Digital Marketing & Social Media Management

SEO, Google Ads, Meta Ads, Instagram growth, LinkedIn strategy, email marketing, and analytics. Many business owners know they need marketing but don't have the time or expertise to do it themselves. Marketing skills trade at a premium because they directly drive revenue.

How to offer it: Show results — even if from your own projects. "Grew my Instagram from 500 to 5,000 in 3 months" is compelling proof. List the platforms and tools you specialise in.

5. Legal, Accounting & Business Advice

GST registration, company incorporation, contract drafting, tax filing, trademark applications, and business planning. Professionals in these fields are among the most sought-after on the platform because their services are expensive in the traditional market. A single hour of legal advice might trade for several hours of web development or design work.

How to offer it: List your qualifications and specific services. Be clear about scope — "30-minute consultation" vs. "full contract review." Specify if there are any legal restrictions on bartering your services.

How to Get Started

Pick one skill you're confident in. Create a concise, honest profile on Grit&Gigs. List what you offer and what you're looking for. Then browse active listings and send a proposal.

You don't need to be an expert. You just need to be competent enough to deliver value. Many of the best barter relationships start with someone saying, "I'm not a professional, but I can help you with that."

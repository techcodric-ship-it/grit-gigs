Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and similar tracking technologies on Grit&Gigs.

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, authenticate your session, and improve your browsing experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies — Required for the Platform to function (e.g., authentication, security).
  • Functional Cookies — Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Analytics Cookies — Help us understand how you use the Platform so we can improve it.
  • Marketing Cookies — Used to deliver relevant advertisements (with your consent).

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Type Purpose Duration
Session Cookies Maintain your session while logged in Session
Authentication Tokens Keep you logged in across visits 30 days
Preference Cookies Remember your settings and preferences 1 year
Analytics Cookies Track usage patterns and improve the platform Up to 2 years

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services (such as analytics providers) that set their own cookies. These services are governed by their own privacy policies.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note that blocking essential cookies may affect the functionality of the Platform.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page.

7. Contact

For questions about our use of cookies, contact us at legal@gritandgigs.in.

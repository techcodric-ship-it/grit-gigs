Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Grit&Gigs — India's skill marketplace.

What is Grit&Gigs? ▾
Grit&Gigs is India's all-in-one skill marketplace where freelancers can sell gigs, barter skills without cash, and find project partners — with escrow-protected payments, UPI/wallet support, and zero commission on barter trades. It combines freelancing, skill exchange, and project collaboration into one platform built specifically for the Indian market.
What is skill barter and how does it work? ▾
Skill barter is trading services directly with another person without exchanging money. On Grit&Gigs, you post what skill you offer and what you need in return. When someone matches your offer, you trade services — your graphic design for their legal consultation, for example. The platform facilitates the match, communication, and delivery tracking, making barter secure and accountable.
How does escrow work for freelancers? ▾
Escrow on Grit&Gigs works by holding the client's payment securely before work begins. The freelancer starts work knowing funds are already secured. Once the work is delivered and the client approves, the payment is released to the freelancer. If there's a dispute, the platform mediates. This protects both parties — clients don't pay for incomplete work, and freelancers don't work without payment assurance.
Is Grit&Gigs a good alternative to Fiverr? ▾
Yes, Grit&Gigs is an excellent Indian alternative to Fiverr. Unlike Fiverr which charges up to 20% commission and is priced in USD, Grit&Gigs operates fully in Indian Rupees, supports UPI and local payment methods, charges lower fees, and offers unique features like skill barter and project collaboration that Fiverr doesn't provide. It's built specifically for the Indian freelancing ecosystem.
Can I trade services without spending money? ▾
Yes. Grit&Gigs has a dedicated skill barter marketplace where you can trade services directly without spending money. Post what you offer and what you need in return, match with another user, and exchange services. The platform handles communication, delivery tracking, and dispute resolution — all without any cash changing hands.
How do I find freelance work in India without experience? ▾
Start by creating a strong profile on Grit&Gigs that highlights your skills even without formal work experience. Offer services at competitive rates initially to build reviews. Use the skill barter feature to trade services with other freelancers — this builds your portfolio and reputation. Join project collaborations to gain practical experience. Many successful freelancers on Grit&Gigs started with barter trades to build their credibility.
What payment methods does Grit&Gigs support? ▾
Grit&Gigs supports UPI, credit/debit cards, netbanking, and wallet payments — all through Razorpay. Payouts to freelancers can be made via UPI or bank transfer. All payments are denominated in Indian Rupees (₹).
How do I get paid on Grit&Gigs? ▾
When a client hires you for a gig, the payment is held in escrow. After you deliver the work and the client approves it, the money is released to your Grit&Gigs wallet. You can then withdraw funds to your UPI ID or bank account. Standard payouts are processed within 1-2 business days.
What skills can I barter on Grit&Gigs? ▾
You can barter virtually any skill — graphic design, web development, content writing, video editing, digital marketing, legal consultation, accounting, music production, tutoring, photography, social media management, SEO, translation, and more. If you have a skill someone needs, you can trade it on Grit&Gigs.
Is Grit&Gigs safe and secure? ▾
Yes. Grit&Gigs uses Razorpay-powered escrow for all financial transactions, meaning payments are only released when work is approved. The platform includes user verification, dispute resolution, secure messaging, and data encryption. Barter trades include delivery tracking and milestone management to ensure both parties fulfill their commitments.
What is project collaboration on Grit&Gigs? ▾
Project collaboration lets you post a project idea and find co-founders, freelancers, or collaborators to build it with you. Describe your project, specify what skills you need, set a budget, and receive bids from interested parties. This is ideal for startups, side projects, and anyone looking to build something with a team.
Is Grit&Gigs free to use? ▾
Yes, Grit&Gigs is free to join. Create a profile, browse gigs, and post barter offers at no cost. Freelance transactions have a nominal platform fee (lower than most competitors), and barter trades are completely free with zero commission. Premium subscription plans are available for additional features.
